Sesame Jellyfish with Chili Sauce

芝麻海蜇 zhīmá hǎizhé / zi1 maa4 hoi2 zit3

From "Food.com"

A traditional Sichuan dish, featuring crunchy jellyfish.

Image by howcheng

Ingredients

  • 1⁄2 lb packaged jellyfish
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp chili sauce
  • 1 small green onion
  • 3 tbsp sesame seeds

Procedure

  1. Prepare jellyfish as directed on package. This step usually takes about three hours. Jellyfish should be dried and desalted before using.
  2. Chop the green onion into small pieces.
  3. Mix the jellyfish, green onion, soy sauce, sesame oil, vinegar, sugar, and chili sauce together in a bowl or other container.
  4. Stir so that all the jellyfish strips are coated with the sauce mixture.
  5. Cover and let sit for at least 30 minutes refrigerated -- the longer the better.
  6. Serve chilled.

Notes

  • Packaged jellyfish can easily be found at most Chinese markets.

Shumai

燒賣 shāomài / siu1 maai2

From "Koisuru Chuugoku"

A popular dish easily found in a dim sun restaurant (茶樓 caa4 lau4), made of egg skin wrapped around pork, fish, or shrimp meat.

Image from Koisuru Chuugoku

Ingredients

  • 24 shumai skins (available at a market)
  • 150g of minced pork
  • 30g of boiled bamboo shoots
  • 1 dried shiitake mushroom
  • 1/3 of a leek
  • 5g of ginger
  • A pinch of coriander if available

Seasoning

  • 2 teaspoons of wine
  • 2 teaspoons of soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sugar
  • A pinch of salt
  • A pinch of pepper
  • A little bit of water

Procedure

  1. Cut up the bamboo shoots, dried shiitake, leek, and ginger into fine bits.
  2. Knead the minced pork and mix with ①.
  3. Place ② inside the shuumai skin and shape the edges into the shape of flower petals.
  4. Steam in a steamer for 6–7 minutes on high.

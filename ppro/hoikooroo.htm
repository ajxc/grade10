Twice-cooked Pork

回鍋肉 huíguō ròu / wui4 wo1 juk6

From "100道最常用家常菜快炒" by 李德全

A spicy pork dish, well-known in China and Japan

Image from Home Cooking Recipe - Kikkouman

Ingredients

  • 120g of minced pork
  • 150g of cabbage
  • 1-2 pimentos (green pepper)
  • 1-4 leeks
  • 1 tbsp of cooking wine
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 1/2 tbsp of cooking oil

Seasoning A

  • 1/2 tbsp of sweet noodle sauce
  • 1 tsp of douban sauce

Seasoning B

  • 1 tbsp of soy sauce
  • A pinch of black pepper

Procedure

  1. Cut the pork into 5cm wide strips.
  2. Finely cut the cabbage, and cut the chili into halves, then 8 pieces.
  3. Chop the leeks into small circles.
  4. Heat 1 tablespoon of cooking oil in a frying pan. Sautee ② and take out when fully covered in oil.
  5. Put ④ in the frying pan. Use the rest of the cooking oil. Sautee the pork. When the color of the meat changes, add the wine and seasoning A and mix.
  6. Add ③ and the garlic. Put back ④, and add seasoning B.

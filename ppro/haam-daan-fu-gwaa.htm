Salted Eggs and Bittermelon

鹹蛋苦瓜 xiándàn kǔguā / haam4 daan2 fu2 gwaa1

From "韓媽媽家常菜"
Image from 韓媽媽家常菜

Ingredients

  • Bittermelon
  • Salted eggs
  • Garlic
  • Green onion
  • Chili pepper
  • Cooking oil

Procedure

  1. Remove the seeds and skin of the bittermelon.
  2. Cut up the salted eggs and mix.
  3. Sautee the garlic and bittermelon.
  4. Mix in the salted eggs, green onion, and chili pepper.

Notes

  • Crushed garlic can be used for those afraid of chili pepper.

Egg Tart

蛋撻 dàntà / daan6 taat1

From "Vancouver Food Blog"

A pastry with origins in pastel de nata, a Portuguese pastry introduced by Christian missionaries in Macau.

Image by stu_spivack

Ingredients

  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon + 2 teaspoon superfine (castor) sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 8 tart shells

Procedure

  1. Crack the egg into the milk.
  2. Add the sugar into the mix.
  3. Beat the mixture until it is smooth.
  4. Pour the mixture into the tarts, leaving 1/4″ at the top.
  5. Pre-heat the oven to 425 F. Bake the egg custard tarts for approximately 10 minutes and the reduce the heat to 400 F. Bake for another 10-15 minutes until the custard has set.

Notes

  • If a knife comes out clean, it means that the tarts are ready.

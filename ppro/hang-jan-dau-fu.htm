Xingren Tofu

杏仁豆腐 xìngrén dòufǔ / hang6 jan4 dau6 fu6

From "Koisuru Chuugoku"

A dessert popular in China and Japan. Despite its name, it is not made of soy, but of ground apricot kernel and sugar.

Image by Iflwlou

Ingredients

  • 1 bag (4g) of powdered agar
  • 2 tbsp of sugar
  • 3/4 cups of milk
  • 150g of of granulated sugar (or sugar)
  • The squeezed juice of 1/2 of a lemon
  • 1/2 of an apple
  • 2 kiwis

Procedure

  1. In a saucepan mix 2 cups of water and 1/2 cups of powdered agar. Boil on high heat, then add sugar and mix for two minutes on medium heat. Mix the milk and agar and freeze for one hour in the refrigerator.
  2. In another saucepan, mix 2 cups of granulated sugar and 1/4 cups of water and melt in medium heat. Add the lemon juice and chill in the refrigerator to make a syrup.
  3. Cut the apple into six 3mm-thick slices. Dispose of the core and leave the skin on. Peel the kiwi and cut into 4mm-thick round slices.
  4. When the jelly hardens, cut into diamond shapes, serve with fruits, and add syrup.

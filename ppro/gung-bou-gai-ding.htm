Kung Pao Chicken

宮保雞丁 gōngbǎo jīdīng / gung1 bou2 gai1 ding1

From "100道最常用家常菜快炒" by 李德全

A spicy Sichuan stir-fry dish made of chicken and chili.

Image by 台灣美食悠遊網

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken thigh
  • 1/2 green onion
  • 2 pimentos (green peppers)
  • 5 chili peppers
  • 80g of peanuts
  • 1 tsp of crushed garlic
  • 1 tsp of crushed ginger

Seasoning A

  • 1 egg white
  • Potato starch
  • A pinch of salt

Seasoning B

  • 1 tbsp of Shanxi laochencu
  • 1 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp of sugar
  • 1 tbsp of cooking wine
  • 1 tsp of sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp of chicken bouillon
  • 1/2 tsp of potato starch

Procedure

  1. Make the chicken bite-sized and cover in seasoning A.
  2. Chop up the green onion and cut the pimentos bite-sized. Cut the chilies in half and take out the seeds.
  3. Cover the chicken, green onion, and pimentos in oil.
  4. Add oil and the chilies to a frying pan and sautee on a low fire until aromatic. Add the garlic and ginger, and mix in seasoning B and ③
  5. Complete by adding the peanuts and sauteeing.

Notes

  • Adding Chinese prickly-ash can also improve the flavor.
  • Chilies burn easily, so remember to sautee on a low fire.

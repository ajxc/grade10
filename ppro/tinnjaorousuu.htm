Qingjiao Rou Si

青椒肉絲 qīngjiāo ròusī / cing1 ziu1 yuk6 si1

From "Koisuru Chuugoku"

A meat dish from Sichuan made of green peppers (青椒 qīngjiāo) and strips of pork (肉絲 ròusī).

Image from Koisuru Chuugoku

Ingredients

  • 200 g of sliced pork
  • 8 pimentos (green pepper)
  • Bamboo shoots (about as much as the pimentos)
  • 8 fresh mushrooms
  • 1 tbsp of ginger
  • Cooking oil (as much as is fit)

Seasoning A

  • 1 tbsp of soy sauce
  • A pinch of salt
  • A pinch of pepper
  • 1 tsp of wine
  • 1/2 tsp of sugar
  • 1 tsp of potato starch

Seasoning B

  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • A pinch of MSG

Procedure

  1. After cutting the meat into 5 mm, add seasoning A.
  2. Cut up the pimento and bamboo shoots.
  3. Add the cooking oil into a frying pan and cook the meat until it changes color. Transfer to a different dish when done.
  4. Add more oil and cook the pimentos, bamboo shoots, and fresh bamboo.
  5. Add the meat and seasoning B.
